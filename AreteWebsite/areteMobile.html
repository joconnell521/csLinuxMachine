Loop Calculator

Loop calc is a mobile app that allows caddies to track their earnings. Caddies can now filter their loops, delete loops, search for a member, and export their loops to a csv. Now that caddies create an account with loop calc, there is no need to worry about ever loosing your loops as you can log in on any IOS device.
